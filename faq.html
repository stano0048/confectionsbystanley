Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?
We are open from 8:00am to 9:00pm on Monday to Friday, 9:00am to 6:00pm Saturdays and 12:00pm to 5:30pm on Sundays and public holidays.
Where are you located?
Our bakery is located at Lenana, opposite The Hub Karen, along Ngong road, Nairobi. You can also find us on Google Maps.
Do you offer delivery services?
Yes! We offer free delivery within Nairobi city and it's neighborhood for orders above KES 3,000. We also deliver across the country. Delivery charges across the country may apply based on location.
Can I place an order online?
Yes, you can order directly from our website and choose either pickup or delivery.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery for in-store pickups.
